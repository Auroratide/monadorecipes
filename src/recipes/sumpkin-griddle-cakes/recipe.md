# Sumpkin Griddle Cakes

Pumpkin-infused pancakes with a hint of vanilla.

* Type: Staple
* Source: Aegaeon's Refined Cuisine
* Prep: 10 minutes
* Time: 30 minutes
* Rarity: 2

## Ingredients

| Ingredient           | Quantity       | Rarity | Type      |
| -------------------- | -------------- | ------ | --------- |
| Flour                | 2 cups         | 1      | Grain     |
| Baking Powder        | 2 teaspoons    | 1      | Grain     |
| Baking Soda          | 1 teaspoon     | 2      | Grain     |
| Salt                 | 1/2 teaspoon   | 1      | Spice     |
| Ground Cinnamon      | 2 teaspoons    | 2      | Spice     |
| Nutmeg               | 1 teaspoon     | 2      | Spice     |
| Pumpkin Puree        | 15 oz can      | 3      | Vegetable |
| Brown Sugar          | 1/3 cup        | 2      | Spice     |
| Egg                  | 1              | 1      | Meat      |
| Vegetable Oil        | 3 tablespoons  | 1      | Oil       |
| Whole Milk           | 1 1/2 cups     | 1      | Liquid    |
| Vanilla Extract      | 1 tablespoon   | 2      | Spice     |

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Set aside. [Flour: 2 cups, Baking Powder: 2 teaspoons, Baking Soda: 1 teaspoon, Salt: 1/2 teaspoon, Cinnamon: 2 teaspoons, Nutmeg: 1 teaspoon]
2. In another bowl, blend the pumpkin, brown sugar, egg, oil, milk, and vanilla extract. Make sure it is well mixed and smooth, at least 1 minute stirring vigorously. [Pumpkin Puree: 15 oz, Brown Sugar: 1/3 cup, Egg: 1, Vegetable Oil: 3 tablespoons, Whole Milk: 1 1/2 cups, Vanilla Extract: 1 tablespoon]
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk gently to combine. The batter should be thick and lumpy.
4. Heat a gridle or skillet over medium heat (about 375°F, 190°C). Coat with butter or nonstick cooking spray.
5. Once hot, drop a large spoonful (1/4 cup) of batter onto the griddle; you can cook as many of these spoonfuls at a time as your pan allows. Cook until holes in the pancake surface start to form around the border, about 2-3 minutes. Then, flip the pancakes and cook on the other side another 1-2 minutes. Repeat until the batter is exhausted.
6. Serve with other breakfast items, like fresh fruit, scrambled eggs, or sausages!


## Interpretation

* Sumpkin: 3
* Weeping Flour: 2
* Dawn Hygrangea: 2

Griddle Cakes are pancakes, and the ingredients are consistent with that: all-purpose flour as weeping flour, and vanilla as dawn hygrangea (since hygrangeas are flowers and vanilla comes from flowers).

Sumpkins are what make this recipe unique. We use pumpkin puree to create an autumn-flavored pancake. Experiment with the spices, and give this Gormotti breakfast dish a try!
